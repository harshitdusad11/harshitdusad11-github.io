My career in finance began in internal audit — an environment that instilled in me an uncompromising standard for financial controls, compliance, and operational integrity. Reporting directly to a CFO, I led audit operations across multiple zones, conducted variance analysis, inventory audits, and MIS reviews, and owned cost analysis, bid computations, and lease-versus-buy evaluations. That foundation shaped everything that followed.

From there I moved into FP&A, spending several years building my core analytical capability inside a large multinational services organisation. I led revenue forecasting, variance analysis, and three-year financial planning across a broad portfolio of business units and geographies — with outputs feeding directly into CEO and CFO-level strategy. I automated the full suite of MIS reports, scorecards, dashboards, and incentive analytics, achieving a step-change in the speed and quality of financial intelligence reaching executive leadership.

In a high-growth global venture, I took full ownership of country-level P&L and cash flow forecasting for a rapidly scaling business — building the Annual Operating Plan, running rolling forecasts, and presenting monthly performance directly to the C-suite. The centrepiece outcome was a $5M profitability improvement delivered through disciplined cost optimisation and margin analysis, alongside full automation of the MIS and management reporting suite.

In my most senior roles, I have operated as a finance transformation leader inside a large, multi-entity global enterprise — taking full ownership of FP&A, month-end close, and executive reporting. I standardised over $500M in balance sheet reconciliations across three entities, reducing financial exposure by up to 95%. I led the substantiation of $826M in capital assets in direct collaboration with audit and executive leadership — with zero material findings. I eliminated $7.5M in accrual liability through vendor and accrual framework redesign, and delivered significant headcount savings through automation and AI-powered process transformation. Throughout, I served as a close strategic partner to senior leadership on five-year planning, headcount analytics, and multi-region rolling forecasts.

Across every role, my purpose has been the same: make the finance function a genuine driver of business performance — not just a recorder of it.
